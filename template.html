Welcome to The Production Group at WGBH

Local knowledge • National experience • World-class facilities

For the last 30 years, The Production Group at WGBH has been providing a wide variety of services for both in-house productions andd outside clients including studio facilities rental, scenics design, post-production, and many others. A highly skilled staff, optimal facilities, and a trusted name make The Production Group the right choice for your next production.

the Outpost

The OutPost suites are available for rental complete with sound isolation booth, high-end monitors, and excellent acoustics. These suites are designed to enhance creative focus by offering complete control over media.

Learn More

the Studios

Our studios have a 21' high fully equipped lighting grid, easy access to adjacent loading docks, green rooms, scenics shop, video/audio control rooms, and event spaces.
